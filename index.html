About Our Gau Shala Initiative

Water is the most critical nutrient for dairy cattle, accounting for 87% of milk composition. The Smart Water Management System system was deployed to ensure zero water scarcity and prevent waterborne diseases among our indigenous breeds. By automating the pump and monitoring Total Dissolved Solids (TDS), we reduce manual labor while maximizing herd health.

Daily Water Requirements

  • Lactating Cows: 100 - 150 Liters/day
  • Dry Cows: 50 - 70 Liters/day
  • Calves: 20 - 30 Liters/day
  • Summer Spike: Needs increase by 30%

Target Water Standards

  • Optimal pH: 6.5 to 7.5 (Prevents acidosis)
  • TDS Limit: Less than 500 PPM
  • Coliform: 0 CFU/100ml
  • Temperature: 15°C to 25°C
